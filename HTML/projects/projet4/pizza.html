Our Story

Traditional Recipes Since 1985

Founded by Italian immigrant Mario Moretti, Mo Pizza has been serving authentic Neapolitan pizza for over three decades. Our secret lies in the perfect combination of traditional techniques and premium ingredients imported directly from Italy.

Every pizza is hand-tossed, topped with the freshest ingredients, and baked in our wood-fired oven to achieve that perfect crispy-yet-chewy crust that pizza lovers crave.
