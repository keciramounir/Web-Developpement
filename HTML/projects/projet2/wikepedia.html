Mounir

Mounir
Pronunciation: moo-NEER
Gender: Male
Origin: Arabic
Meaning: "Shining", "Illuminating"
Related names: Munir, Muneer

Mounir (Arabic: منير) is a masculine given name of Arabic origin meaning "shining" or "illuminating". It is a common name in Arabic-speaking countries and among Muslim communities worldwide.

Etymology

The name Mounir derives from the Arabic root N-W-R (ن-و-ر), which relates to light and illumination. It is the active participle form of the verb "anara" (أنار), meaning "to illuminate" or "to light up". The name carries connotations of enlightenment, brightness, and guidance.[1]

Variations

The name has several variations across different languages and regions:

  • Munir - Common alternative spelling
  • Muneer - Another English transliteration
  • Munira - Feminine form of the name
  • Nur/Noor - Related names from the same root meaning "light"
